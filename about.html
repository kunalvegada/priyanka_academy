About Me

I am a music teacher with 5-6 years of experience in teaching Indian classical, semi-classical, bhajans & ghazals, Gujarati (Garba) , Hindi, Marathi folk songs , Bollywood songs on piano.

Indian Classical Music: Highlighting my expertise in teaching Indian classical music.
Semi-Classical Music: My teaching approach for semi-classical genres like thumri, dadra, and other light classical forms. Emphasize the fusion of tradition and innovation.

Bhajans & Ghazals: My passion for devotional music.Will help and guide students in expressing emotions through bhajans & ghazals.
Garba Gujrati-Folk Songs: My role in preserving cultural heritage by teaching Garba and traditional Gujarati folk songs.

Hindi & Marathi Folk Songs: Highlighting my ability to connect with students through regional folk songs.
Bollywood Songs on Piano: My approach to teaching Bollywood songs playing & singing on the piano.
